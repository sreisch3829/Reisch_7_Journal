Paid License- liscense that is paid
Shareware- software that is shared between two people or more
Trialware- software that is only available during its trial
Ad-ware- a software that helps generate money from ad
Crippleware- being able to use software until you want to save (then you need a registration key)
Freemium- a program or software that is free but additional features cost money
FreeWare (vs Free-OpenSource)- Free-Opensource can be distributed everywhere but Freeware is free but has no redistribution allowed
