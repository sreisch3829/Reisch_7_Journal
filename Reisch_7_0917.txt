Five different Java types are:
int: used for whole numbers, short for integer
float: 32 bits, used for decimals
short, long, double: 16, 64, 64 bit, also used for decimals
Char: short for characters (ie: "A"), called letters, numbers, and punctuation
string: collection of chars "90ej"
boolean: true/false, on/off, 0/1
Generally all are used for numbers, letters, and punctuation.
